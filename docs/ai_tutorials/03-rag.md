# Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

## What

RAG combines retrieval (vector search) with LLM generation to produce context-aware answers.

---

## Why

- LLMs lack up-to-date or domain-specific knowledge
- Reduces hallucination
- Enables custom knowledge injection
